<h1>Athletica</h1>
<li>Every individual must hold a valid college identification card.</li>

<li>In case of team entries, all team members must be from the same college.</li>

<li>Every individual brings its own its own personal gear.</li>

<li>Common equipments to be used in the competition will be provided by ISM Dhanbad.</li>

<li>Maximum 3 entries are allowed for each event from one college and one participant can do maximum of 3 events apart from relays.</li>

<li>Whatever is to be used in the competition has to be verified by the referees.</li>

<li>Depending upon the number of participants, events can have heats, semi-finals and/or direct finals.</li>
