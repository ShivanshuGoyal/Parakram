ABOUT

Two words won't define Parakram, but it still is our Annual Sports Meet, held every year in the colds of February. This season, pay us a visit, flex your muscles, show your strength, be a rage, make your game and winning shall take care of itself.
We ISM invite you to our cold soil, fill it with the thump of your breath, let this steam burn you.
Show your PARAKRAM.
moreless
No pain, no palm. No thorns, no throne. No gall, no glory. No Cross, no Crown.
Let this heat get on you, let the steam burn you, let the sweat drown you and let this game define you.
The time is back, the board is set. Some grips, striving for your touch. Some acts, waiting for your guts. Some us and some you looking down the rest.
People call us meagre; Call us the petty side-life of their prosperity. But we, We don’t buy them, because this is much beyond their might, much beyond the compilation of their bucks, much beyond the sense of their mind.
Once again, let your warm feets touch the cold soil of our tracks; fill the hollowness of our wind with the thump of your breath. Minds around us galore, much words thrown; Enough said and done.
Show the spirit, be a Rage; This February, WeISM, invites you and everyone, every player and every playing soul, to this exhilarating and scintillating world of Sports and Games at our place. Our ISM, your battlefield. To one of the biggest sporting event in East India, to the culture.
Let your Parakram reach the zenith of its survival, the year of 2013, ISM. This PARAKRAM, Let the games begin.
Parakram is Our Annual Sports Meet, conducted at the Inter-College level, organised by the very own students of ISM. A 3-day extravaganza, Parakram, is not our definition of sports, we don’t wish to; Parakram is an honour to the oldest trait of human survival. The platform sets every year in the month of February, at Indian School of Mines, Dhanbad, Jharkhand. Our comprehensive organisation peeks from the fact that the sports included are Cricket, Basketball, Football, Tennis, Table-Tennis, Squash, Volleyball, Badminton, Athletics, Gymnastics. Besides the core sporting events, there are always the other, but never minor, events like Sports Quiz and other Fun-filled stakeouts.
So, this winter, come join us in the city of Dhanbad, flex your muscles and we promise that you will be challenged to your last breath and any preconceived notion about what it takes to be an athlete will be put to test.
